Projects

Virtual Pet

This project utilized object-oriented programming, test-driven development, and agile methodology. Our team developed a console application that allowed a user to create and interact with Tamagotchi-style "virtual pets" in a menu-driven, game-type format. Players could create and print out all the pets. Players could interact with pets to feed, play with, and give them medicine, affecting their internal states of hunger, boredom, and sickness. Players were able to admit pets into a "virtual pet shelter", list out all the pets in the shelter, and remove pets from the shelter. A tick method simulated the passage of time. As time ticked by, the pet's status changed, affecting its hunger, boredom, or sickness.
